Hello!

Yuan (you-ann) Stevens is a third year law student at McGill University. As a cyberfeminist researcher with a keen interest in technology, privacy, and the law, she is passionate about helping others think critically about technology and its ability to facilitate activism and disrupt legal systems.

She has previously worked for the European Center for Constitutional and Human Rights and the Centre for Internet and Human Rights.

She is currently a research assistant for hacker and computer culture expert Gabriella Coleman. You can find her on Twitter and Linkedin.
